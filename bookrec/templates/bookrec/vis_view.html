How does the ZK Book Recommender know what books to recommend?



First, the dataset is loaded into a dataframe. This allows the program to easily search through the data and find your book.
Once your book is found, it's time to do some comparisons!
The program searches through the dataframe and finds every book that matches yours.
It looks for matching authors, genres, Goodreads ratings, and page counts.
For every match it finds, it adds the book's ID to a special list.
Once all the matches are confirmed, the program sorts the list to see how many matches each book recieved.
It then tallies up all the points, and the top 6 books are our winners!
